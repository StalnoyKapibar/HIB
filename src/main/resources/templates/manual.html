To make purchases, you need to register in the online store. To register, you must click on the "Sign in" link. An authorization window will open, in which you should click on the "Register new profile" link.

Next, you will be taken to the page for entering registration data. After entering the registration data, click on the "Sign up" button. Check the email address that was provided during registration. A letter with your registration data has been sent to the specified address.

The choice can be made in the following ways:

- by clicking on the "categories" tab and selecting the contents of the category section of interest. Categories are on the left side of the screen.

- by entering a request into the search box located on each page of the online store.

- using advanced search by clicking on the "search page" link and entering the parameters of interest

The language can be changed by clicking "Settings" and choosing the required language.
